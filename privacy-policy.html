Introduction

Welcome to Mind Bloom, your magical garden of puzzles and challenges! This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application.

By using Mind Bloom, you agree to the practices described in this privacy policy. If you do not agree to these practices, please do not use our application.

1. Information we collect

We collect the following information to improve your gaming experience:

  • Game Data: Your progress, scores, completed levels, collections, and game preferences
  • Technical Data: Information about your device, operating system, application version, and unique identifiers
  • Usage Data: Playtime, features used, errors encountered, and interactions with the application

2. How we use your information

We use your information for:

  • Providing and improving our gaming services
  • Personalizing your gaming experience
  • Analyzing application usage to optimize it
  • Displaying personalized advertisements (via Google AdMob)
  • Ensuring security and preventing fraud
  • Communicating with you regarding updates and new features

3. Data sharing

We never sell your personal data. We may share your information only in the following cases:

  • With third-party service providers (such as Google AdMob for advertisements)
  • When required by law or to protect our rights
  • With your explicit consent

4. Data storage and security

Your game data is stored locally on your device and synchronized with our secure servers. We retain your data as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with our legal obligations.

5. Cookies and similar technologies

Our application uses tracking technologies to improve user experience and display relevant advertisements. These technologies include cookies, advertising identifiers, and tracking pixels.

6. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw your consent at any time

7. Third-party advertisements

Mind Bloom uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. These ads may use cookies and tracking technologies to personalize advertising content. You can disable ad personalization in your device settings.

8. Children's data

Our application is designed for all ages. If you are under 13 years old, please ask your parents' permission before using our application. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Important changes will be communicated through the application or by email. We encourage you to regularly check this page to stay informed about our practices.

10. Legal basis

We process your personal data based on your consent, contract performance (provision of gaming service), and our legitimate interests (service improvement and security).
